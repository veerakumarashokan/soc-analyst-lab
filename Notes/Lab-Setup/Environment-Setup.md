## Lab Design

### SOC Lab
- Log monitoring  
- Alert analysis  
- Incident response simulation  

### Penetration Testing Lab
- Basic attack techniques (WiFi, scanning, enumeration)

### Networking Lab
- Packet Tracer simulations  
- Network topology design and troubleshooting
